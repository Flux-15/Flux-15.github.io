01 — About

Building the future,
one circuit at a time

I'm Om Sanjaykumar Patel — a third-year Electronics & Communication Engineering student at Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU), Gandhinagar. My journey in engineering didn't start at a university — it started with curiosity. From early schooling in Bharuch to completing a Diploma in Electrical Engineering at Shri K.J. Polytechnic, I've always preferred building things over just reading about them. That hands-on instinct led me to robotics, embedded systems, and eventually, research.

Today, I work across the full stack of autonomous systems — from designing custom flight controller PCBs on STM32H7 to implementing hybrid PSO-A* algorithms for robot path planning, to publishing research on VLC-based swarm coordination and quantum-secured optical networks. I've interned at Dexsent Robotics, worked in the robotics research lab at PDEU, and am currently a Research Intern at Nav Wireless Technologies.

Beyond research, I lead with intention. I serve as General Secretary of the IEEE RAS PDEU Student Branch Chapter, PRO & Outreach Head for Team Cretus, and Technical Head at Brahmand. I believe engineering leadership means building community as much as it means building systems.
